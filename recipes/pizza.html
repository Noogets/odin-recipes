Pizza Recipe

How to make pizza just like your grandmother's!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup of pizza sauce
  • 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup of sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup of sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup of sliced mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup of sliced green bell peppers

Instructions

  1. Preheat the Oven

    2. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven while it preheats to get it nice and hot.

  2. Prepare the Dough

    3. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness. If you like a thin crust, roll it out thinner; for a thicker crust, keep it a bit thicker. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza peel or a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.

  3. Add the Sauce and Toppings

    4. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce, then add your desired toppings such as pepperoni, olives, mushrooms, and bell peppers.

  4. Bake the Pizza

    5. Carefully transfer the pizza to the preheated oven (or onto the pizza stone if using). Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted. Keep an eye on it to prevent burning.

  5. Serve and Enjoy

    6. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade pizza!
